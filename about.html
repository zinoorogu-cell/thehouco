About The HOU Co.

Built on Trust,
Grounded in Experience

Over 14 years of building, scaling, and leading businesses across Nigeria's most dynamic industries. This is what we bring to you.

The Founder

Hassan Olawunmi Uthman

Hassan O. Uthman is a Marketing and Business Consultant with over 14 years of experience building and scaling businesses across Nigeria's most dynamic sectors — entertainment, finance, technology, and retail.

His career has taken him from operator to strategist, from Lagos boardrooms to international stages, giving him a uniquely holistic view of what it takes to succeed in the Nigerian market. Based across Nigeria and the United Kingdom, Hassan brings both insider knowledge and global perspective to every engagement.

He has led marketing for some of Nigeria's most recognised brands, built teams from the ground up, and developed strategies that have delivered measurable, lasting growth. Today, through The HOU Co., he channels all of that expertise into helping diaspora Nigerians turn their business ambitions into reality.

Our Purpose

Why The HOU Co. exists

The HOU Co. exists for Nigerians in the diaspora who want to build meaningful, successful businesses in Nigeria but need trusted guidance, real insight, and on-ground support.

Along the way, Hassan has created a trusted team that knows how to move, build, and deliver across borders — and developed an extensive network of operators, partners, and specialists who understand what it truly takes to build businesses in Nigeria.

Our Values

What drives everything we do

Every engagement is guided by four non-negotiable principles that have defined our work across 14 years.

Clarity Over Complexity

We Hear You

Most importantly, we understand the fear

Nigerians in the diaspora carry very real concerns when thinking about investing or building a business back home. We've heard every version of this fear. We understand it — because we've seen what happens when people try to build without the right support.

The fear of being misled by people who don't have your best interests at heart

The fear of choosing the wrong partner and losing everything you've worked for

The fear of investing your time and money and seeing no results

The fear of not knowing who to trust in a market you're not physically in

The fear of starting something you cannot physically oversee from abroad

"The HOU Co. exists to remove that fear. We give you clarity, structure, strategy, and on-ground support so you can build your business confidently from anywhere in the world."
